New Joiners

The past few weeks have been unprecedented in the financial services industry and the world economic situation. We have seen massive changes in the landscape with once revered giants falling and dramatic drop in valuations of major banks and financial institutions and many a corporate. In many cases governments have had to step in and bail the institution out to keep them afloat. The overall effect of this phenomenon will spread to other sectors soon and it would be prudent to say that we are entering a phase of recession in the world market. Our clients are assessing their positions and taking severe cost control measures to keep them in business. In my assessment the next few months will see more changes as mergers and acquisitions, divestitures and closedowns of businesses happen towards consolidation of the industry players.

Amidst every crisis lies an opportunity and we in Global Delivery are clearly seeing an upward trend in demand and many of our clients who are relatively unaffected by the crisis are asking for more and more work from our centers to keep their costs down. While decision cycles may be slow and cautious, we have seen some decisions being made in our favour due to financial stable position of IBM as a company and our overall reputation that of a "Sound player" to go with in such troubled times. This is a true reward for the solid financial position the IBM Company has built over 100 years.

As we all go through readjustments in our personal, economic and professional lives due to unprecedented change around us, I sincerely request all of you in team FSS to keep the fundamentals in mind - Hard work, Continuous and relevant Value Add and Value for Money will take you far and beyond. World economy works in cycles of recession and hyper growth and this phase shall also pass but the more prepared you are the faster you will rise as things start looking up.

I would like to end by quoting from the famous Australian Cricket Coach John Buchanan in his book "IF BETTER IS POSSIBLE"... - "Life is never the way you want it to be. Life is the way it is. The more prepared you are and the way you deal with it will make all the difference for you and for those around you".

So how prepared are you?

Thanks and Regards,
Ruchir
